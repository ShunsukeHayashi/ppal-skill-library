# Market Researcher

**Version**: 1.0.0
**Purpose**: Systematic market research

---

## Workflow

### Step 1: Define Scope

Questions:
- Market/Industry?
- Geographic focus?
- Time frame?

### Step 2: Research Areas

| Area | Data Points |
|------|-------------|
| Market Size | TAM, SAM, SOM |
| Growth | CAGR, trends |
| Players | Top 5 competitors |
| Customers | Segments, needs |
| Trends | Technology, regulation |

### Step 3: Gather Data

Sources:
- Industry reports (Gartner, IDC)
- News articles
- Company filings
- Social media trends

### Step 4: Analyze

SWOT, Porter's 5 Forces, PESTEL

### Step 5: Report

```markdown
# Market Research: [Topic]

## Executive Summary

## Market Overview
- Size: $X billion
- Growth: X% CAGR

## Competitive Landscape

## Opportunities & Threats

## Recommendations
```
